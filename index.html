Water Usage

Overall, 1025/1029 Woolman used the largest amount of water with 5236 gallons, while 1015/1017 Woolman consumed the least amount with 80 gallons.

In February 2024, the average water usage in all units was approximately 2994 gallons. This is up from an average usage in January of about 2185 gallons. We challenge you to be at or below 500 gallons per person per month.

Electricity Consumption

For electricity, 1021 Woolman consumed the highest amount of electricity, and the least was used by 1045 Woolman.

The electricity usage for all Campus Village units in February 2024 averaged at 813 kWh. This is down from 1202 kWh in January 2024. This is likely due to warmer weather conditions requiring less electricity for heating. We are working to reduce our campus carbon footprint, and electricity is a big piece of that. We challenge you to reduce your usage a bit each month and to be below 250 kwh per person.

Conservation Tips

Lastly, don't forget to take a look at three useful tips for water and electricity conservation we highly recommend.

Small actions matter!

Adjust thermostat settings to conserve energy. Keep at or below 70F for heating and at or above 74F for cooling to reduce heating and cooling costs.

Unplug electronics and chargers when not in use to prevent "phantom" energy usage. Consider using power strips to easily switch off multiple devices at once.

Utilize natural light during the day and minimize reliance on artificial lighting to save electricity.
